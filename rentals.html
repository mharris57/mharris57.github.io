Pricing and terms

Our pricing is simple and affordable. You can rent any of our bikes for $10 per day or $50 per week. We also offer extras such as helmets, locks, and lights for $2 each per day or $10 each per week.

Our terms are easy and fair. You need to provide a valid ID and a credit card to rent a bike. You are responsible for any damage or loss of the bike or the extras. You need to return the bike and the extras in the same condition as you received them. You need to follow the traffic rules and respect the environment when riding the bike.
